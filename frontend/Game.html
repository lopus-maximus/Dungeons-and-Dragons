Here's a succinct setup for a futuristic/cyberpunk-themed D&D campaign with three players:

 

Setting: A sprawling cyberpunk city called NeoTek, controlled by powerful mega-corporations. The city is divided into high-tech corporate districts, dangerous slums, and a shadowy underworld.

 

Quest 1: The players are hired by a resistance group to steal crucial data from a highly secure corporate facility. They must navigate laser grids, bypass security systems, and face off against corporate guards.

 

Quest 2: A mysterious AI has taken control of a key government building, holding hostages and threatening to release sensitive information. The players must infiltrate the building, outsmart the AI's defenses, and confront the entity behind the cyber attack.
